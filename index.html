About us

We are Cool Flow — an acoustic music collective born in the heart of Manila, Philippines. What started as late-night jam sessions between friends has grown into a passionate project dedicated to stripping songs down to their emotional core. We obsess over the intersection of melody and emotion, crafting covers and originals that feel like sunset conversations by the beach.

Our sound spans from classic rock to modern indie and OPM — always focused on raw vocals, warm guitars, and that intimate feeling you get around a campfire. Every cover we create is an opportunity to reinterpret a song through our own lens, finding new depth in familiar melodies and sharing stories that resonate with anyone who has ever loved music.

Whether we are performing on a sandy shore at golden hour or recording in a dimly lit room, our mission remains the same: to make music that feels like home. We believe the best songs do not need layers of production — just honest voices, steady hands on strings, and the courage to be vulnerable. From viral OPM hits to forgotten 90s gems, we chase the feeling of connection that only live acoustic music can deliver.
